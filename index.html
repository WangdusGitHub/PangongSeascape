  • Pangong Tso

    Pangong Tso Lake, also known as Pangong Lake, is a spectacular saltwater lake situated at an elevation of 4,350 meters (14,270 feet) on the Indo-Tibetan border. It spans approximately 160 kilometers in area, with one-third lying within Indian territory and the remaining two-thirds in China. The lake’s serene beauty, color-changing waters, and picturesque landscapes make it a must-visit destination in Leh-Ladakh.🏞️🌅
